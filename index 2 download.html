Can I cancel my subscription?

Cancellation Policy for Crafty Dev Technologies Services

Domain Registration:

  1. Cancellation Requests: Clients must submit cancellation requests for domain registration services in writing, indicating the domain name and reason for cancellation.
  2. Refund Eligibility: Refunds are subject to the terms of the domain registrar. Some domain registrations may not be eligible for refunds once processed.
  3. Transfer Assistance: Clients may request assistance with transferring the domain to another registrar. Additional fees may apply.

Hosting Services:

  1. Cancellation Notice: Clients must provide a minimum of 30 days' notice for cancellation of hosting services.
  2. Refund Policy: Refunds for prepaid hosting fees are prorated based on the remaining unused period. No refunds are provided for the current billing cycle.
  3. Data Backup: It is the client's responsibility to back up their data before cancellation. Crafty Dev Technologies is not liable for any data loss post-cancellation .

Maintenance Services:

  1. Cancellation of Maintenance Plans: Clients may cancel maintenance plans at any time by providing written notice.
  2. Refund Eligibility: Refunds for prepaid maintenance services are prorated based on the remaining unused period. No refunds are provided for the current billing cycle.
  3. Project Completion: Any ongoing maintenance tasks or projects will be completed up to the cancellation date. No new tasks will be initiated post-cancellation.

General Terms:

  1. All cancellation requests must be sent to Admin .
  2. Crafty Dev Technologies reserves the right to deny or delay cancellation requests that do not comply with the specified procedures.
  3. Any outstanding fees or charges must be settled before the cancellation is processed.
  4. Crafty Dev Technologies is not responsible for any third-party fees associated with domain transfers or cancellations.

By availing of Crafty Dev Technologies services, clients acknowledge and agree to abide by these cancellation terms and conditions. Crafty Dev Technologies may update these terms at its discretion, and clients will be notified of any changes.

How can I manage my account?

Managing your account with Crafty Dev Technologies is a straightforward process. Follow these steps to ensure smooth account management:

  1. Login to Your Account: Visit our website and locate the "Login" or "Account" section. Enter your credentials (username/email and password) to access your account.

  2. Dashboard Access: Once logged in, you'll be directed to your account dashboard. Here, you'll find a summary of your services, invoices, and account settings.

  3. Update Personal Information: Navigate to the "Profile" or "Account Settings" section to update your personal information, including contact details, billing address, and password. Ensure your information is accurate for effective communication.

  4. Review and Pay Invoices: Check your "Invoices" or "Billing" section to review current and past invoices. Pay any outstanding invoices through the provided payment methods. Set up automatic payments for convenience if available.

  5. Manage Services: In the "Services" or "Products" section, you can view details of your subscribed services. If you wish to upgrade, modify, or cancel a service, follow the prompts provided. Be sure to review the terms and conditions for any service adjustments.

  6. Domain Management: For domain-related actions, such as renewals, transfers, or DNS management, navigate to the "Domains" section. Follow the instructions to manage your domain settings.

  7. Support and Help Center: Should you need assistance or have questions, check the "Support" or "Help Center" section. Here, you can find guides, FAQs, and contact options to reach our support team.

  8. Security Measures: Regularly update your password and enable two-factor authentication if available. This enhances the security of your account.

  9. Communication Preferences: Manage your communication preferences to receive updates, newsletters, or important announcements. This can usually be found in the "Profile" or "Account Settings" section.

  10. Logout Securely Always log out of your account after managing your services to ensure the security of your information.

If you encounter any difficulties or have specific inquiries about managing your account, feel free to contact our support team at We're here to help!

Is my credit card information secure?

Absolutely. At Crafty Dev Technologies, we take the security of your information very seriously, especially when it comes to sensitive data like credit card information. Here are the measures we have in place to ensure the security of your credit card details:

  1. Secure Transmission: All communication between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols. This ensures that any data, including credit card information, transmitted during transactions is secure.

  2. PCI DSS Compliance: We adhere to Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) compliance. This is a set of security standards designed to ensure that all companies that accept, process, store, or transmit credit card information maintain a secure environment.

  3. Tokenization: Credit card details are tokenized, meaning that the actual card information is replaced with a secure token during transactions. This ensures that even if intercepted, the data is meaningless to unauthorized parties.

  4. Restricted Access: Access to your credit card information is restricted to authorized personnel only. Our staff undergoes regular training on data protection and privacy to maintain the highest standards of security.

  5. Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits and assessments to identify and address any potential vulnerabilities. This proactive approach helps us stay ahead of evolving security threats.

  6. Third-Party Security: Any third-party payment processors we use also adhere to stringent security standards, providing an additional layer of protection for your credit card information.

  7. Transparent Policies: Our privacy policy clearly outlines how we handle and protect your data. We encourage you to review this policy to understand the measures we take to safeguard your information.

If you have any specific concerns or questions regarding the security of your credit card information, please don't hesitate to reach out to our support team at support. We are committed to providing a secure and trustworthy experience for our clients.
